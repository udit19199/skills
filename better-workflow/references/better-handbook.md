# Better Handbook Reference

Use these norms when writing updates, handoffs, and collaboration messages for Better work.

## Values To Encode

- Honesty: surface mistakes, blockers, and uncertainty early.
- Independence: solve problems without handholding, but ask for help with evidence.
- Deliver commitments on time: be explicit about commitments and completion.
- Team player: make work legible to teammates and give constructive feedback.

## Remote Work

- Keep working hours visible and current.
- Maintain at least 4 overlapping collaboration hours with the immediate team.
- Prefer project channels over DMs for work context.
- Prefer written communication. Write enough detail to remove guesswork.

## Daily Updates

Morning update:

- Plan: outcomes and deliverables for the day.
- Problems: blockers or risks.

EOD reply:

- Progress: completed outcomes with PRs, tickets, docs, screenshots, logs, or other evidence.

Bad updates list activity without outcomes. Good updates name artifacts and results.

## Meetings

- Attend key meetings: standup, exit, retro, all hands.
- Be on time.
- Listen first.
- Share agenda and context before calling a meeting.

## 1:1 Prep

Bring:

- Top 1-2 priorities since last 1:1.
- Blockers.
- Wins or milestones.
- Feedback or suggestions.
- Questions about growth, goals, conflict, or motivation.
